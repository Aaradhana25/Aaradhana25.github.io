ABOUT

Established in 2010, Dulcéa has been a sanctuary for coffee lovers and food enthusiasts. Our journey began with a simple passion for exceptional coffee and a desire to create a space where community, conversation, and culinary excellence could thrive together.

  • Food & Wine Best New Chef (2017)

Marcus Chen

Pastry Chef

Marcus Chen is the artistic genius behind Dulcéa's beloved pastry program. With a background in fine arts and culinary training from the Institute of Culinary Education in New York, Marcus approaches pastry as both science and art form.

Awards & Recognition

  • National Pastry Championship Gold Medal (2018)
  • World Chocolate Masters Finalist (2020)
  • Dessert Professional Top 10 Pastry Chefs (2021)
  • Author of "Sweet Geometry" cookbook (2023)

Fernandez

Head Barista

As Dulcéa's Head Barista, Olivia Fernandez brings passion, precision, and deep knowledge to every cup. Born into a family of Colombian coffee growers, she has coffee in her DNA and has dedicated her career to elevating the craft of coffee preparation.

Awards & Recognition

  • National Barista Championship Finalist (2017, 2018, 2019)
  • World Barista Championship Top 10 (2020)
  • Specialty Coffee Association Certified Trainer
  • Featured in "Coffee Masters" documentary series (2022)

Sofia Patel

Culinary Director

As Dulcéa's Culinary Director, Sofia Patel oversees the strategic direction of all culinary operations across our multiple locations. With an MBA in Hospitality Management and classical training from the Culinary Institute of America, Sofia brings a unique blend of business acumen and culinary expertise.

Awards & Recognition

  • Hospitality Leader of the Year (2018)
  • Culinary Innovation Award, Global Gastronomy Summit (2020)
  • Sustainability Champion, Restaurant Leadership Conference (2022)
  • Author of "Future Food: Sustainable Cuisine for a New Era" (2023)
